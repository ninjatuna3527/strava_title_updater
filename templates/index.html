How it works

One small automation with maximum flair.

01

Connect Strava

Authorize using Strava OAuth. Your Strava password is never handled by this app.

02

Detect activities

When a fresh activity arrives, HanRide chooses a random Chinese script title.

03

Rename the moment

The activity title is updated automatically, giving your feed instant mythic energy.

Privacy

Built to do one job.

HanRide should request only the Strava permissions needed to read incoming activities and update titles. Keep access tokens encrypted, avoid logging personal activity data, and provide a simple disconnect route before launch.
