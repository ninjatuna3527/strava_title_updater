How it works

Your stats go in. A suspiciously good excuse comes out.

01

Connect Strava

Authorize using Strava OAuth. Your Strava password is never handled by this app.

02

Read the effort

When a fresh activity arrives, HanRide uses its type, duration, and distance as context.

03

Generate the excuse

AI writes one short, playful excuse and HanRide automatically applies it to the activity.

Privacy

Built to do one job.

HanRide requests only the Strava permissions needed to read incoming activities and update their titles. Activity type, duration, and distance are used to generate the title; your Strava password is never handled by the app.
